## Phase 1: Document verification (before interview)

| # | Check | What we look for | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Valid ABN** | Look up on [abr.business.gov.au](https://abr.business.gov.au). Must be ACTIVE. Must be registered in THEIR name or their company name — not someone else's ABN. | 🔴 MANDATORY — no ABN = no engagement |
| 2 | **GST registration** | Check on ABR. If sub earns >$75K/year they MUST be GST registered. Not registered + above threshold = red flag (not lodging BAS). Under $75K = optional. | 🟠 CHECK |
| 3 | **Public Liability Insurance** | Request Certificate of Currency. Current (not expired). Covers bathroom resurfacing/regrouting specifically. **$10M minimum** (AU industry standard for property work). $20M preferred. ⚠️ No PL = no engagement. One slip / chemical spill / damaged property = your liability. | 🔴 MANDATORY |
| 4 | **Personal accident / income protection insurance** | Sole traders NOT covered by workers comp for themselves. If they injure themselves on your job + can't work, they may try to claim against YOU. Request evidence of personal accident or income protection. Not legally mandatory but strongly recommended. | 🟠 RECOMMENDED |
| 5 | **Workers Comp (if employees)** | If sub has ANY employees (even casual), they MUST have NSW workers comp via [iCare](https://icare.nsw.gov.au). Request Certificate of Currency. Sole trader with no employees → not required for themselves. | 🟠 IF APPLICABLE |
| 6 | **NSW Subcontractor's Statement** | Statutory form. Sub declares they've paid all wages, super, workers comp premiums, payroll tax for the work period. ⚠️ **Per s175B Workers Compensation Act 1987 (NSW), if you DON'T collect this, YOU become liable for their unpaid workers comp premiums.** Collect with EVERY payment, not just onboarding. Use standard NSW form. | 🔴 MANDATORY — every payment |
| 7 | **Identification** | AU driver's licence or passport. Confirms person matches ABN holder. Prevents fraud (someone using another's ABN). | 🔴 MANDATORY |
| 8 | **Portfolio of work (5-10 before/after photos)** | Regrout subs: close-ups of grout lines, silicone corners. Resurface subs: full bath/tile shots showing finish quality. No portfolio = no experience = no trial. | 🔴 MANDATORY |
| 9 | **References (2-3)** | From previous clients or companies. **Call them.** Ask: "Would you use them again?" "Quality issues?" "On time?" "Clean up properly?" | 🔴 MANDATORY |
| 10 | **Google / online presence** | Search name + business. Check complaints, negative reviews, NCAT records. Check ABN history — <12 months operating = higher risk. | 🟠 CHECK |

## Phase 2: Interview (phone or video, 30 min)

| # | Question | Good answer | Red flags |
|---|---|---|---|
| 11 | How long doing regrout/resurface work? | 2+ years full-time. Specific job examples. Knows product names. Worked for company before (Jim's, Surface Care, Bathroom Werx). | <1 year. Vague answers. Doesn't know products. "I watched YouTube." |
| 12 | What products/systems do you use? | **REGROUT**: names cement + epoxy grouts (Davco Sanitized, Ardex FG8, Laticrete PermaColor). Knows when to use each. **RESURFACE**: names pro coating systems (Hawk Glass-Tech, similar 2-part). Describes multi-coat process. | "Just Bunnings stuff." Names White Knight or Dulux Renovation (consumer-grade, peels in 1-2 years). Can't describe coating process. |
| 13 | Walk me through process for a standard shower regrout | Should mention: grout removal (multi-tool/rake), cleanup, joint prep, mixing, float application, wiping, silicone removal/reapplication, cure-time advice. **KEY: cure time mentioned.** | Skips steps. Doesn't mention silicone separately. Says "1 hour" (impossible — 3-5hr min). Doesn't mention cure time. |
| 14 | Walk me through process for bath resurface | Should mention: assessment, chip/crack repair, acid wash, sanding, masking/protection, ventilation, primer (with dry time), topcoat (multi-coat with dry time), inspection, cure-time advice (48-72hr). **KEY: masking + ventilation + respirator + cure time.** | Skips prep. No masking. "2 hours" (impossible — 4-8hr min). No ventilation/respirator/cure time mentioned. |
| 15 | What goes wrong + how do you prevent it? | **REGROUT**: cracking (wrong mix), haze (poor cleanup), silicone lifting (poor prep). **RESURFACE**: peeling/bubbling (poor prep + moisture), orange peel (bad spray distance), dust in finish (dirty room). Good subs KNOW because they've fixed others' mistakes. | "Nothing goes wrong." = inexperienced or careless. |
| 16 | Own tools + vehicle? | Yes to both. Lists specifics. Work van/ute with material storage. | No vehicle. Borrows tools. **Needs us to supply materials = sham contracting risk** (would not be a genuine independent contractor). |
| 17 | Work for other companies / other clients? | **YES — critical for sham contracting protection.** Ideal: "I work for [other company] and take direct jobs." | "No, I'll just work for you." 2024 Fair Work Act "whole of relationship test" — exclusive engagement = sham risk. |
| 18 | How do you handle callback / warranty claim? | "I go back + fix at no charge. It's my reputation." Has had callbacks + resolved. Understands callbacks happen even to good tradies. | "Never happens to me" / "That's customer's problem." **Do not hire** — they'll fight every callback, you pay twice. |
| 19 | Comfortable with photo documentation? Before/during/after every job? | "Yes, already do that for my own records." | "Why do you need photos?" Resistance = hiding sloppy work. |
| 20 | Days/hours available + jobs per week? | Establishes capacity. Regrout sub: 1-2/day. Resurface sub: 1/day typical. | Only 2 days/week + no other clients = not serious OR sham risk. |

## Vetting outcome decision

```
All 🔴 MANDATORY items pass + 🟠 items satisfactory + interview answers solid
                                ↓
                    ✅ PROCEED to Paid Trial Job
                    (see docs/sop/sub-trial-job-protocol.md)

Any 🔴 fails OR multiple 🟠 fails OR interview red flags:
                                ↓
                    ❌ Do not proceed. Document why. File for future reference.
```

## Document storage

For every sub vetted (pass or fail):
- ABN screenshot from ABR
- PL Certificate of Currency (PDF)
- Personal accident / income protection cert (if available)
- Workers comp cert (if applicable)
- ID (driver's licence / passport — store securely)
- Portfolio photos (5-10 before/afters)
- Reference call notes
- Interview notes
- Vetting outcome decision

**Store in**: shared Google Drive folder per sub: `Subs/{SubName-FullLegalName}/Vetting/`

**Retention**: 7 years per ATO + Fair Work record-keeping requirements.
