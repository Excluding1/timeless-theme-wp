## 6. Step-by-step recipe

### Day 1 — Regrout + Bath prep

**Phase 1: Arrival + assessment (15min)**
1. SMS sent "30min ETA"
2. Greet, walk through 2-day plan with customer
3. 5 photos before: shower interior + grout close-up + bath full-length + basin + bathroom wide
4. **STOP triggers:**
   - Asbestos suspected (pre-1990 + tile damage)
   - Bath material unexpected (acrylic/fibreglass — different SKU)
   - Tiles loose / bath delamination
   - Customer wants extra (tap fix, tile resurface) NOT in quote → office requote

**Phase 2: Whole-bathroom setup (30min)**
1. Drop sheets in 2 zones (shower work + bath work)
2. Vent fan + window open
3. Mask: bath rim if working shower-side, basin if working shower-side, tap surrounds

**Phase 3-6: Shower regrout (per RSC-02 phases)**
*See [RSC-02 § 3-6](RSC-02-shower-regrout-standard-cement.md) — same procedure: old grout removal, mould treatment, new grout, silicone replacement.*

**Phase 7: Bath prep (60min)**
1. Inspect bath: chip count, surface condition
2. Mask: bath rim, plug, overflow, tap base, surrounding tile (3M Blue)
3. Sand bath surface 120 grit (oscillating multi-tool)
4. Vacuum dust thoroughly
5. Wipe Hawk Industrial Cleaner → meths → acetone (final degrease)
6. Bath ready for Day 2 (DO NOT prime today — let mask cure overnight)

**Phase 8: Day 1 wrap (30min)**
1. 4 photos during: post-grout + post-silicone + masked bath + final state of shower
2. Customer comm: "Day 1 done. Shower curing — no use 24hr. Bath ready for Day 2."
3. SM8 update: Day 1 phase complete

### Day 2 — Bath + Basin resurface

**Phase 9: Recheck (15min)**
1. Confirm overnight cure of grout / silicone (light touch)
2. Refresh masking on bath if any tape lifted
3. Confirm bath surface clean (no dust settled overnight)

**Phase 10: Bath resurface (per BTH-01 phases)**
*See [BTH-01 § Phase 6-7](BTH-01-bath-resurface-standard.md):*
1. Mix Ultragrip primer + catalyst
2. Spray 2-coat primer (15min flash between)
3. Mix Glas-Tech topcoat + catalyst + reducer
4. Spray 2-3 coat topcoat (15min flash between)

**Phase 11: Basin resurface (60min)**
1. Mask basin (overflow, drain, tap surrounds, surrounding vanity)
2. Sand basin 120 grit + meths wipe
3. Spray Ultragrip primer (light coat)
4. Spray Glas-Tech topcoat (2-3 coats per Hawk system)

**Phase 12: Day 2 cleanup + handover (60min)**
1. Carefully remove masking while topcoat tacky (clean lines)
2. 6 photos final: shower wide + shower corner detail + bath full + bath tap end + basin close-up + bathroom wide
3. Leave Card 1 (Regrout) + Card 2 (Resurface) per [customer-aftercare-cards.md](../../templates/customer-aftercare-cards.md)
4. Customer walk-through:
   - Shower: 24hr cure (already curing since Day 1)
   - Bath + basin: 48-72hr no use
   - Use ONLY non-abrasive cleaners going forward
   - 5-year warranty (private home) on bath/basin / 2-year grout / 1-year silicone
5. Photo upload to SM8 (15 photos: 5 before + 4 during + 6 after)
6. Mark complete
