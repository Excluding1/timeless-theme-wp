## 1. Customer-facing summary

**Description:**
> We repair a single chip in your bath surface — fill, sand smooth, then colour-matched coating to blend invisibly with surrounding surface. Quick fix; ready to use in 12-24 hours.

**T2 scope:**
- Single chip up to 30mm diameter
- Fill with auto body filler / chip-repair compound
- Sand smooth (multiple grits)
- Colour-matched topcoat (white or bone)
- 1-year warranty

**NOT included:**
- Multiple chips (CHR-02 for 2-5 chips)
- Cracks or structural damage
- Full bath resurface (BTH-01)

## 6. Step-by-step recipe

### Phase 1: Arrival + assessment (5min)
1. 2 photos before: chip close-up (with ruler/coin for scale) + bath wide (colour reference)
2. **STOP triggers:**
   - Chip larger than expected (>30mm) → office requote (CHR-02 multi)
   - Underlying crack visible → CRK-01 territory, requote
   - Bath material acrylic with chip going through → can't repair, needs replacement

### Phase 2: Mask + setup (10min)
1. Tape around chip (2cm border) to define repair zone
2. Light drop sheet under bath for spray catch

### Phase 3: Filler (5min + 15min cure)
1. Apply auto body filler / Hawk compound to chip
2. Smooth with putty knife — slightly proud of surface (will sand flush)
3. Wait 15min for filler to cure (passive — sub can prep tools)

### Phase 4: Sand flush (10min)
1. 180 grit on sanding block — work circular, even pressure
2. Test flush with finger — should be undetectable
3. Switch to 320 grit for final smoothness
4. Wipe with methylated spirits — degrease

### Phase 5: Topcoat (10min)
1. Mix small qty Glas-Tech topcoat (colour-matched — white or bone per assessment)
2. Spray light coat over repair area + 5cm feathered edge
3. Allow 5min flash
4. Spray second coat — slightly larger area to blend

### Phase 6: Cleanup + handover (5min)
1. Carefully remove tape while topcoat tacky
2. 2 photos after: chip area close-up + wide bath (showing blend)
3. Quick aftercare card (Card 3 — quick service variant)
4. Customer walk-through: 12-24hr cure, gentle cleaning, watch for staining first 7 days
5. Photo upload to SM8 + complete

## 7. Quality criteria

| Criterion | Good | Fail |
|---|---|---|
| Surface flush | Undetectable to finger | Bump or dip detectable |
| Colour match | Blends invisibly at arm's length | Visible spot, wrong shade |
| Edge feathering | No visible edge of repair | Hard line where repair ends |
| Cleanup | No overspray, tape residue | Visible spray edge, residue |
