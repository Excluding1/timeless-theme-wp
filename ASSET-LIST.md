## SHOT LIST — What to Photograph on Every Job

When completing real jobs, capture these shots every time:

### Before Photos (take FIRST, before any work starts)
1. Wide shot of entire bathroom
2. Close-up of damage (mould, cracks, stains, peeling)
3. Close-up of problem area from different angle
4. Any water damage evidence

### During Photos
5. Masking/preparation in progress
6. Technician applying primer or coating
7. Equipment/tools being used
8. Mid-process showing the transformation happening

### After Photos
9. Same angle as photo #1 — wide shot of entire bathroom
10. Same angle as photo #2 — close-up of repaired area
11. Detail shot of the finish quality
12. Customer standing next to result (with permission — testimonial photo)

### Video
13. 15-second timelapse (prop phone up, record entire process, speed up)
14. 5-second customer reaction clip (optional, with permission)

> **Critical:** Match EXACT same angle and lighting for before/after pairs.
> Use natural light where possible. Clean the lens. Landscape orientation for web.
