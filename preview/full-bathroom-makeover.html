Full Bathroom Makeover FAQs

Common questions about our complete bathroom makeover service.

Our complete bathroom makeover includes bath resurfacing, wall and/or floor tile resurfacing, full regrouting, silicone replacement at all junctions, vanity cabinet respray, and basin restoration. Everything your bathroom needs to look brand new — without demolition. You can also choose individual services if you don’t need the full package.

Most standard bathrooms are completed in 2-3 days. Your bathroom is usable again within 48 hours of completion. Compare that to 3-6 weeks for a full renovation — with no demolition, no plumber, and no mess.

Absolutely. You can choose colours for the bath coating, tile resurfacing, grout, vanity respray, and basin. We’ll help you select a cohesive palette that transforms the entire space into something modern and fresh.

No. You can stay in your home the entire time. We work room by room and the only disruption is not using the bathroom being worked on for 24-48 hours while coatings cure. If you have a second bathroom, there’s practically zero impact on your daily routine.

The main difference is that we work with your existing fixtures (bath, tiles, vanity) rather than replacing them, which is why it’s a fraction of the cost.
