How We Restore Your Basin

A proven 5-step process from assessment to final cure.

01

Assessment

Inspect basin condition, identify chip depth, staining severity, and recommend chip repair vs full resurface.

02

Clean & Prep

Basin cleaned, degreased, and sanded. Surrounding area masked and protected.

03

Chip Repair

Any chips filled, shaped, and smoothed. Colour-matched to the existing surface.

04

Resurface

Multiple coats of professional-grade coating applied. White, bone, or custom colour finish.

05

Final Cure

Light use after 24 hours, full cure in 48 hours. Result: a smooth, glossy, like-new basin.

Basin Restoration FAQs

Common questions about basin restoration in Sydney.

Basin restoration starts from $320 inc GST for a full resurface. Chip repair only starts from $230. Best value when combined with other bathroom work like bath resurfacing or shower regrouting — as setup and travel are shared across services.

Most basin restorations are completed in 1–3 hours. The basin can be used again after 24 hours to allow the coating to fully cure. When done as an add-on to bath resurfacing, it adds minimal time to the overall job.

We restore porcelain, ceramic, acrylic, cast iron, and composite basins. Pedestal, wall-mounted, drop-in, undermount, and vanity-top styles are all suitable. If the basin is structurally intact, we can restore it.

Basin restoration is still cheaper than replacement as a standalone job, but offers the best value as an add-on to other bathroom work. When combined with bath resurfacing, shower regrouting, or tile resurfacing, setup and travel costs are shared — making it exceptional value.
