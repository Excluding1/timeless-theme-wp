Why You Shouldn’t Ignore It

A small chip today becomes a bigger problem tomorrow.

trending_up

Damage Spreads

Chips grow over time. Thermal stress from hot and cold water widens cracks. Cleaning products seep into exposed areas. What starts as a small nick becomes a large gouge.

coronavirus

Hygiene Issues

Chipped surfaces harbour bacteria, mould, and soap scum in the rough, exposed areas. In a basin you use daily for handwashing and teeth cleaning, that’s a health concern.

water_damage

Water Damage Below

On vanity-mounted basins, water seeping through chips can damage the timber or MDF vanity cabinet below. Swelling, rot, and mould growth in the vanity are expensive to fix.
