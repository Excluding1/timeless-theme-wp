Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Timeless Resurfacing (ABN 30 412 161 602) is a partnership operating a bathroom resurfacing business in Sydney, NSW. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and website visitors in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

This policy explains how we collect, use, store and disclose your personal information when you use our website, submit a quote request, or engage our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact details — your name, email address, phone number and street address, submitted through our contact or quote request forms.
  • Bathroom photos — photographs of your bathroom, shower, bath or tiles that you submit for quoting purposes.
  • Website usage data — information collected automatically through cookies and analytics tools, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited and time spent on our site.
  • Communication records — emails, phone call notes and messages exchanged between you and our team.

We only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary for our business functions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide accurate quotes for bathroom resurfacing, regrouting and related services.
  • To communicate with you about your enquiry, quote or booked job.
  • To schedule, carry out and follow up on our services.
  • To improve our website, services and customer experience.
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

  • Personal information is stored securely in password-protected systems.
  • Access to your information is limited to authorised personnel who need it to perform their duties.
  • We use industry-standard security measures including encryption for data transmitted through our website.

When we no longer need your personal information for any purpose outlined in this policy, we will take reasonable steps to destroy or de-identify it.

5. Third Parties

We may share your personal information with the following third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business:

  • Google Analytics — for website traffic analysis and usage statistics.
  • GoHighLevel CRM — for managing quote requests, customer communications and follow-ups.
  • Payment processors — for processing payments securely when you pay for our services.

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and data protection obligations. We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third party.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to help us understand how visitors use our site and to improve your browsing experience.

Cookies we use include:

  • Analytics cookies — to measure website traffic and user behaviour (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Functional cookies — to remember your preferences and improve site performance.

You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.

7. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access — request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that we correct any inaccurate, out-of-date or incomplete information.
  • Opt out — unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal information, subject to any legal obligations requiring us to retain it.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 9 below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Bathroom Photos

Photographs of your bathroom submitted through our quote request forms or via email are used solely for assessing your job and providing an accurate quote.

We may wish to use before-and-after photos of completed work for marketing purposes (such as our website gallery or social media). We will only do so with your explicit written consent. You are under no obligation to agree, and it will not affect our service or pricing.

If you have given consent and later change your mind, simply contact us and we will remove the photos promptly.
