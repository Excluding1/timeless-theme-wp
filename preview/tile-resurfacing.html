Our Process

How We Resurface Your Tiles

Every step exists for a reason. Here’s exactly what we do and why it matters for a flawless, long-lasting finish.

search
Step 1

Assessment & Surface Check

We inspect every tile for cracks, loose edges, and adhesion. We check the grout condition too — if grout needs replacing, we recommend doing both at once for the best result.

priority_high Why it matters: Resurfacing over cracked or loose tiles means the coating cracks too. We fix the foundation first.

cleaning_services
Step 2

Deep Clean & Etch

Tiles are degreased, cleaned of soap scum, mould, and mineral buildup. Then chemically etched to create a surface the coating can grip. This is especially important on glazed tiles which are designed NOT to absorb anything.

priority_high Why it matters: Glazed ceramic tiles are the hardest surface to bond to. Without etching, any coating will peel. This step makes or breaks the job.

build
Step 3

Repair & Fill

Any cracked tiles, chipped edges, or damaged grout lines are repaired with epoxy filler and sanded flush. The surface needs to be perfectly smooth before coating — every imperfection shows through.

priority_high Why it matters: A resurfaced tile is only as good as the surface underneath. Filling imperfections now prevents them showing through the new finish.

format_paint
Step 4

Primer & Colour Coat

Specialist bonding primer is applied, followed by the architectural-grade colour coat in your chosen finish. We spray-apply using HVLP equipment for an even, professional result — no brush marks, no texture.

priority_high Why it matters: This is where your dated brown tiles become clean, modern white. The spray technique gives a factory finish that brush or roller simply can’t match.

verified
Step 5

Clear Coat & Seal

A protective clear coat is applied over the colour for extra durability and UV resistance. Grout lines are sealed. 24–48 hour cure time, then your bathroom is ready.

priority_high Why it matters: The clear coat adds a protective shell that resists scratches, stains, and yellowing. It’s what keeps the finish looking fresh for years, not months.

cleaning_services

No Mess Guarantee

We contain the work area with drop sheets, vacuum all dust as we go, and leave your bathroom cleaner than we found it. You won’t find dust or residue in your hallway.
