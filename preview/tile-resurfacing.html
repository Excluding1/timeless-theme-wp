Tile Resurfacing FAQs

Common questions about tile resurfacing in Sydney.

Tile resurfacing starts from $1,210 inc GST for a standard shower area. Full bathroom walls, floors, and splashbacks are priced based on total square metreage. We provide fixed-price quotes based on your photos — no hidden fees, no surprises.

Yes — we resurface ceramic, porcelain, textured, patterned, and mosaic tiles. Small mosaic tiles (under 50mm) may incur a surcharge due to additional surface preparation needed. Send us photos and we’ll confirm suitability and pricing.

Wall tiles last 10–15 years with proper care. Floor tiles last 5–7 years due to foot traffic, with anti-slip coating applied for safety. We back every job with a 3-year coating warranty for your peace of mind.

Absolutely. We have over 900 colours available including stone-fleck granite look finishes. White, ivory, and light grey are the most popular choices, but we can match any colour you like — even custom blends.

Yes — the coating covers both tiles and grout lines, creating a seamless, easy-to-clean finish. This is one of the biggest benefits: no more scrubbing grout. If you prefer visible grout lines for a traditional tile look, we can mask them during application.
