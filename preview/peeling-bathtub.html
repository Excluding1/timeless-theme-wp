Why It’s Peeling — And Why It Won’t Stop

Peeling coating doesn’t stabilise. It gets worse. Here’s what happened and what comes next.

format_paint

Previous DIY Failed

Hardware store bath paint is single-part, brush-on, and not designed for wet environments. It peels within 6–12 months — every time.

layers_clear

Poor Surface Prep

The original coating never bonded properly because the surface wasn’t etched, cleaned, or primed correctly. Water gets underneath and lifts it.

trending_up

Gets Worse

Rough edges catch and peel wider with every use. Water finds new gaps. The peeling area doubles in size every few months.
