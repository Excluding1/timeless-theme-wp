Types of Damage We Fix

From small nicks to deep gouges — if your bath is chipped, we can repair it.

brightness_alert

Small surface chips from dropped bottles or daily use. Quick to fix, invisible when done.

crisis_alert

line_end

border_style

Chips along the bath rim or edges from impacts. Rebuilt and shaped to match the original profile.

Why You Shouldn’t Ignore It

A small chip today becomes an expensive problem tomorrow.

warning

Rust Spreads

On steel and cast iron baths, an exposed chip lets water reach the metal underneath. Rust spreads beneath the enamel, causing it to flake and crumble — eventually requiring a full resurface or replacement.

water_damage

Water Damage

On acrylic baths, chips allow water to seep into the material, causing bubbling, discolouration, and structural weakening over time. What starts as a $230 repair becomes a $3,000+ replacement.

trending_up

Gets Worse Over Time

Every chip grows. Thermal expansion and contraction from hot water widens cracks. Cleaning products get into exposed areas. The longer you wait, the bigger the job.

How We Repair Your Bathtub Chip

A proven 5-step process for a seamless, invisible repair.

01

Clean & Prep

Area cleaned and degreased. Loose material removed, surface etched for adhesion.

02

Professional-grade polyester or epoxy filler applied in layers, building up from the base.

03

04

Sand & Blend

Progressive wet-sanding from coarse to ultra-fine. Surface feathered into surrounding area.

05

Topcoat colour-matched to your bath. Sealed with a durable, waterproof clear coat.
