Common Floor Grout Problems

These are the floor grout issues we fix every week across Sydney. If yours looks like any of these, we can have it sorted in a day.

Cracked & Crumbling Floor Grout

Grout cracking between your bathroom or laundry floor tiles? Chunks coming loose when you sweep? Foot traffic, thermal expansion, and building movement cause floor grout to crack faster than wall grout. Every crack lets water through to the substrate. We grind out the damaged joints and fill with fresh sanded grout rated for foot traffic.

  • check_circle Sanded grout rated for heavy foot traffic
  • check_circle Stops water penetrating to the substrate
  • check_circle No tiles removed or replaced

Discoloured & Stained Floor Grout

Floor grout started white but now it’s brown, grey, or patchy? Years of mopping pushes dirty water into porous cement grout. Cleaning products leave residue that builds up in the joints. Foot traffic grinds dirt deeper every day. We replace the stained grout entirely so every joint is uniform, bright, and sealed.

  • check_circle Every joint removed and replaced fresh
  • check_circle Uniform colour across the entire floor
  • check_circle Epoxy option resists future staining permanently

Mouldy Floor Grout

Black spots in your bathroom floor grout that keep coming back after cleaning? Mould grows inside porous cement grout, not just on the surface. In bathrooms and laundries where the floor stays damp, bleach only kills what you can see. The roots survive inside the grout and regrow within weeks. Full regrout is the permanent fix.

  • check_circle Full grout removal, not just a surface clean
  • check_circle Epoxy option eliminates future mould growth

Not sure if your floor grout needs cleaning or full regrout? Wondering about epoxy vs cement for floors? These are the questions we hear most. If yours isn’t here, send us a message. No pressure.

Every floor is different. The cost depends on floor area, tile size, grout type (epoxy or cement), and current condition. Send us 3-4 photos and we’ll have a fixed-price quote back within hours. The number you see is the number you pay.

Most bathroom floors are completed in 4-8 hours (one day). Larger areas like combined bathroom and laundry may take a second day. The floor can be walked on 24-48 hours after completion.

If the grout is structurally sound but just discoloured, professional cleaning plus sealing may be enough. If it’s cracked, crumbling, or tiles are moving, full regrout is the only lasting fix. We’ll tell you which you need after seeing photos.

Epoxy is ideal for floors. It’s non-porous, never needs resealing, and resists staining from mopping and foot traffic. Cement grout works but needs resealing every 1-2 years. Most homeowners skip resealing, which accelerates failure.
