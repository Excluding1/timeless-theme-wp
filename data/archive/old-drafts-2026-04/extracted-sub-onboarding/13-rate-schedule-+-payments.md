**R46 C2**: 4. Team verifies
job quality

**R46 C3**: Team reviews after photos. Checks for any customer complaints. Confirms job meets quality standard.

If quality issue: notify sub, withhold payment pending rectification, issue payment schedule within 10 business days (SOPA requirement).

**R46 C4**: Within 2 business days
of invoice receipt

**R46 C5**: GHL + ServiceM8

**R47 C2**: 5. Invoice approved
for payment

**R47 C3**: Once quality verified + Subcontractor's Statement received → invoice approved in Xero.

Create a weekly batch: approve all invoices received that week for payment on Friday.

**R47 C4**: Approved within 3-5
business days of receipt

**R47 C5**: Xero

**R48 C2**: 6. Payment processed

**R48 C3**: EFT bank transfer via Xero batch payment.

Weekly batch payments every Friday. This is how Jordan does it — subs know they get paid every Friday for work completed the previous week.

Alternatively: pay per job within 7 days of invoice. Simpler at low volumes.

**R48 C4**: Weekly Friday batch
OR within 7 days
of approved invoice

**R48 C5**: Xero → bank EFT
