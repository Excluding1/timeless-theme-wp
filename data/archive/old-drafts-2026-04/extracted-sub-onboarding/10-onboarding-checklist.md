**R19 C4**: Brief sub on Clause 23 (data/privacy): customer details are confidential, only used for assigned jobs, must not be retained after job completion, must not be shared. Note the briefing date.

**R20 C2**: ☐

**R20 C3**: Variation process
briefing

**R20 C4**: Brief sub on Clause 21: if they find unexpected issues on site (hidden mould, worse condition), they must STOP, notify you with photo + text, and wait for written approval before proceeding with any extra work.

**R21 C2**: ☐

**R21 C3**: Professional conduct
briefing (Clause 26)

**R21 C4**: Walk sub through Clause 26 in plain language:
• Arrive clean and on time. Notify if >15 min late.
• No smoking on customer property. No substances. Zero tolerance.
• Stay in the work area only. Don't enter other rooms.
• Don't discuss pricing — "all pricing goes through the office."
• Don't offer additional services or hand out your own details.
• Don't bring anyone else to the job without approval.
• Leave the bathroom cleaner than you found it.
Get verbal acknowledgment. Note date.

**R22 C4**: Explain: "If you accept a job and the customer cancels last minute, you get a $100 callout fee — we cover that. But if you accept a job and then cancel on us with less than 48 hours notice, there's a $100 fee the other way. Fair's fair." Also explain the 15-minute wait rule for locked properties.

**R23 C2**: ☐

(Clause 30)

We'll requote the customer. You get paid based on the actual job, not the photo estimate. Never quote the customer yourself."
