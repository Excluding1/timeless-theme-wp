**R13 C2**: SUB CATEGORIES — WHO YOU'RE HIRING

**R14 C2**: Category

**R14 C3**: Details

**R15 C2**: REGROUT SUB
(cement + epoxy)

**R15 C3**: Skills: Remove old grout, clean joints, apply cement grout and/or epoxy grout, replace silicone in corners/junctions, mould treatment.
Tools: Oscillating multi-tool or grout rake, grout float, silicone gun, sponges, masking tape, bucket.
Materials they supply: Davco, Ardex, or equivalent grout (cement + epoxy), Selleys or Parfix silicone, mould killer.
Typical experience: Tiler background or specialist regrout/silicone tradesperson.
Licence required: None — regrouting is maintenance, not building work.
Typical day rate (what you pay them): $350-$600 depending on job size.

**R16 C2**: RESURFACE SUB
(coatings specialist)

**R16 C3**: Skills: Surface preparation (acid wash, sanding, degreasing), masking/protection, spray application of multi-coat systems (primer + topcoat), chip/crack repair, colour matching.
Tools: HVLP spray gun or airless sprayer, respirator, extraction fan, masking materials, sanding equipment.
Materials they supply: Hawk Glass-Tech system (via ARS) or equivalent professional coating. NOT White Knight or Dulux Renovation Range.
Typical experience: Automotive spray painting background, kitchen/bathroom resurfacing specialist, or trained by a resurfacing company.
Licence required: None for cosmetic resurfacing. If waterproofing is involved → licensed waterproofer required.
Typical day rate: $400-$800 depending on job complexity.

**R17 C2**: DUAL SUB
(regrout + resurface)

**R17 C3**: Rarest and most valuable. Can do a full bathroom refresh in one visit. Higher day rate justified. Vet both skill sets separately — being good at regrouting doesn't mean they can spray.
Typical day rate: $500-$900.

**R18 C2**: SPECIALIST SUB
(niche only)

**R18 C3**: Examples: Epoxy-only specialist, stone fleck/decorative finish specialist, commercial-only sub, chip repair-only.
Useful for overflow or niche jobs. Don't need to be full-time. Can be on-call.
Pay: Per job, not day rate.
