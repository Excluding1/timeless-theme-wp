**R14 C2**: COST ANCHOR
(one sentence)

**R14 C3**: "A full bathroom renovation typically costs $15,000–$30,000.
Yours is done for a fraction of that — in just one day."

**R14 C4**: ANCHORING: $15K-$30K makes $1,660 feel tiny.
GAIN FRAME: "Just one day" emphasises ease.
This one sentence may be the highest-leverage line in the entire email.

**R15 C2**: 3 WHAT'S INCLUDED
BULLETS (max)

**R15 C3**: ✅ All labour + professional-grade materials
✅ Ready to use within 24-48 hours
✅ Written guarantee on all work

**R15 C4**: FLUENCY: THREE bullets, not eight. Research shows 3-5 items is optimal.
CERTAINTY: "Written guarantee" = risk reversal.
Keep this SHORT — they should be clicking, not reading.

**R16 C2**: HOW IT WORKS
(3 steps)

**R16 C3**: 1️⃣ Pay a small 10% deposit (${{custom.deposit_amount}})
2️⃣ Pick your preferred date
3️⃣ We do the work — you pay the rest after

**R16 C4**: PAIN OF PAYING: "Small 10%" + "pay the rest after" = barely registering as a cost.
COMMITMENT LADDER: 3 tiny steps, not one big leap.
STEP 3: "You pay after" = they're in control. Massive trust builder.

**R26 C2**: BEFORE/AFTER PHOTO PAIR
(hero image)

**R26 C3**: Side-by-side before/after of a SIMILAR completed job.
Caption: "Here's a {{custom.area_selected}} we did in [nearby suburb] last week"

If possible, match the service type (regrout, resurface, etc.)

**R26 C4**: SOCIAL PROOF: Visual proof > any words.
SIMILARITY BIAS: Same area, same suburb, same service = "if it worked for them, it'll work for me."
This is the #1 conversion driver for people who didn't pay on first contact.

(3-4 sentences max)

FLUENCY: Short. Casual. Not salesy.
SINGLE CTA: One button. One action. Don't dilute.
