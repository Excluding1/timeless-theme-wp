**R3 C2**: This is the page that turns browsers into paying customers. Built as GHL Documents & Contracts → Proposal template.

**R5 C2**: ⚡ DESIGN RULE: One scroll on mobile. No PDFs. No downloads. No pages. Price visible without scrolling. Deposit button visible within 2 thumb-scrolls.

**R7 C4**: Psychology + Why It's Here

**R8 C2**: 1. HEADER
(instant credibility)

**R8 C3**: Logo + "Your Bathroom Quote"
Quote #{{document.number}} | {{current_date}}
Prepared for: {{contact.first_name}}
Valid until: {{document.expiry_date}}

**R8 C4**: PERSONALISATION: Their name = this is FOR them, not a template.
SCARCITY: "Valid until" date creates real deadline.
PROFESSIONALISM: Quote number = legitimate business.

**R9 C2**: 2. THEIR PHOTOS
(the magic section)

**R9 C3**: Display 2-3 of THEIR submitted photos in a row.
Heading: "Here's what you showed us"
Subtext: "We've reviewed your photos and here's our recommendation."

**R9 C4**: ENDOWMENT EFFECT: Their bathroom in a professional context = they own this process.
IKEA EFFECT: They took these photos. Validated effort = attachment.
TRUST: "We actually looked at YOUR bathroom" — most competitors don't do this.
⭐ THIS IS THE #1 DIFFERENTIATOR.

**R10 C2**: 3. WHAT WE'LL DO
(scope — keep it short)

**R10 C3**: Dynamic based on service. Example for regrout:
"Our technician will:
• Remove all old grout
• Clean and prep every joint
• Apply fresh mould-resistant grout
• Replace all silicone seals
• Clean up — ready to use in 24hrs"

**R10 C4**: CERTAINTY: Specific steps = they know exactly what happens.
FLUENCY: Bullet points here are OK — this is a checklist, not prose.
CONTROL: "Ready to use in 24hrs" = they know the timeline.

**R11 C2**: 4. WHAT'S INCLUDED
(kill objections)

**R11 C3**: ✅ All labour and materials
✅ Travel to {{custom.suburb}}
✅ Clean-up on completion
✅ Written [X]-year guarantee

"No hidden costs. The price you see is the price you pay."

**R11 C4**: RISK REVERSAL: "No hidden costs" kills the #1 fear.
CERTAINTY: Written guarantee = safety net.
The "no hidden costs" line should be BOLD — it's the most important trust signal on the page.

**R17 C2**: 10. PAYMENT OPTIONS
(reduce friction)

**R17 C3**: 💳 Pay online (button above)
📱 Pay by phone: [number]
🏦 Bank transfer: BSB [XX] ACC [XX] Ref: {{document.number}}

**R17 C4**: CHOICE: Some people don't trust online payments. Giving alternatives reduces "I can't pay" as an objection.
Keep this SMALL — it's a fallback, not the primary path.

**R18 C2**: 11. FINE PRINT
(bottom, small)

**R18 C3**: • This is a fixed-price quote, valid for 7 days from {{current_date}}
• A 10% deposit secures your booking date
• Deposit is non-refundable if you cancel less than 48 hours before the scheduled work date, as our technician and materials will already be allocated
• Cancellation with 48+ hours notice: your deposit is transferred to a new date at no extra cost
• Balance due on completion, once you're satisfied with the work
• All prices include GST. ABN: [XX XXX XXX XXX]
• Any additional work discovered on-site will be quoted separately and requires your written approval before proceeding
• Your consumer guarantee rights under Australian Consumer Law are not affected by these terms
• [Your Business Name] Pty Ltd | [Phone] | [Email]

**R18 C4**: NSW/ACL COMPLIANCE:
• "Less than 48hrs before scheduled work date" — clear timing, not ambiguous.
• 10% is reasonable/proportionate to actual loss (sub booking, materials).
• Reschedule option shows good faith.
• "Written approval" for variations — ACL requirement.
• "Consumer guarantee rights not affected" — MANDATORY under ACL.
• Unfair contract terms are now ILLEGAL (2023 amendment). These terms pass.
