**Action**: Add to FUTURE-PLAN as **F14**. Methodology for adding adjacent services without ramping our own subs. Steps:

4. Receive lead → contract out to specialist sub from competitor research
5. Make small margin, keep ball rolling
6. If unit economics work → formalise; if not → kill cleanly

This is high-leverage for expansion. Note: less ethical-cool ("stole someone's business"), more "leverage existing trade networks." We frame it as "we provide better customer experience + admin so quality subs win more work."
